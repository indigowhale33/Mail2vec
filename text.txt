GwonJae,

Unfortunately, because of your absence, the earliest you can start your
process is August 26th, 2016.
The catch is that the MAVNI program is a temporary program, set to expire
at the end of Fiscal Year 2016 (September 30th, 2016). There aren't any
words on the extension of the program, but in the past it has been extended
almost every year since 2009.

I would recommend that you apply for OPT if you already haven't (unless
you're going to grad school). If you know where you will be around August
26th, find a recruiter in the area using this website:
http://www.goarmy.com/locate-a-recruiter.html
Call and ask for a recruiter who is familar with the MAVNI program, and if
they seem to have little knowledge about the program, try other recruiters
in the area. I would call right around beginning of August so you can
prepare the necessary documents.
If you'll be in Williamsburg during that time, definitely give a call to
their recruiting center at (757) 259-9750 and ask for Sergeant Hendricks.
He will take care of you.

As far as documents needed, from what I know: Your I-20, I-94, Passport,
Visa, Social Security Card (they will help you get one if you don't have
one yet, but this can really slow down your process), High School
Transcript in English, W&M official transcript and diploma, Birth
Certificate (in Korea, this is 영어로 번역 및 공증된 기본증명서), , and any documents
related to significant medical history, or legal history (this includes
speeding tickets, and so forth).

If you have all of those and you get lucky, you might just be able to
enlist by September 30th. If not, I hope that the MAVNI program gets
extended for FY 17.

I wish you the best of luck in your last semester.

Sincerely,
Patrick
